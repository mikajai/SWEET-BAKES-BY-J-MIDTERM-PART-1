Address

Seating Capacity

Our original Imus branch offers a cozy setting, comfortably accommodating approximately 20-25 guests.

Parking Status

Limited street parking is available. Paid parking can be found in nearby commercial areas.

Brief History

Sweet Bakes by J humbly began its story in 2020 on Rizal Street in Imus. Starting as a small home-based passion project by Jairrah, the delightful aroma of her croissants and pastries soon drew in neighbors and friends. As demand grew, this charming storefront was established, becoming the very first Sweet Bakes by J. It remains a cherished spot in Imus, known for its authentic flavors and the warm community it fostered from the very beginning.

Brief History

Building upon the success of its initial venture, Sweet Bakes by J expanded to the vibrant student hub near España Boulevard in Manila. This branch, established in 2023, quickly became a favorite among students and locals seeking delicious and affordable baked goods. Known for its freshly baked breads and budget-friendly treats, the España location continues to serve as a convenient and welcoming spot for a quick bite or a sweet indulgence in the heart of Manila.

Seeking to offer a delightful escape alongside its delectable treats, Sweet Bakes by J established its Tagaytay branch in 2025. Continuing the tradition of quality baking, the Tagaytay branch offers a wider menu, often featuring seasonal items that complement the local Tagaytay experience, making it a sweet stop for both locals and tourists alike.
